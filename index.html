Our Methodology

Value / Fundamental Investing

Deep analysis of company financials, earnings power, management quality, and intrinsic value before any capital is deployed.

Quantitative + Qualitative Research

Combines hard numbers (P/E, P/B, ROE, ROA, gearing ratio, Margins),  with qualitative factors like competitive moat, industry position, and macro backdrop.

Prudent Risk Management

Zero tolerance for speculative trading. Positions sized carefully. No leveraged instruments. Downside protection is as important as upside capture.

Recognised for Excellence

Startup 6.0 winner

Awarded to us by Nile University of Nigeria

Startup 6.0 is the 6th edition of the annual student entrepreneurship and innovation event at the Nile University of Nigeria. The program empowers student founders by providing them with funding, mentorship, and a platform to pitch their business ventures to investors and industry experts.

We recently took the stage at Nile University’s Startup 6.0, and what a journey it turned out to be for us as a team. Building DOE Compounders Club from an idea into a winning startup was easily one of the most intense, challenging, yet incredibly rewarding experiences our team has ever faced.

Every latenight strategy session and pitch iteration forced us to grow, but more importantly, it gave us the perfect stage to test our vision in the real world with real people. We absolutely loved the process, the grit, and the energy it took to stand out.

Winning the SME category was beyond just a trophy to us; it proved something we’ve always believed in, that the Nigerian youth are ready. There’s a massive, undeniable hunger among young Nigerians to understand the financial markets, master long-term investing, and take control of their financial futures. Seeing that passion firsthand has completely fueled our drive.

This is just the beginning for us, and we are more committed than ever to preaching financial literacy and Investing all across Nigeria.
